Garnier Men Virtual Product Launch

Activation - Live Production - Graphic Design - VR Event Hire

Objective :
Organize a new product launching event exclusively for distributors via a private online channel due to COVID-19. The goal was to ensure it was easy to join, interactive, and provided direct access to product information.

Activation Strategy:
Led the end-to-end planning and execution of a distributor-exclusive virtual product launch during the COVID-19 pandemic. My role included sourcing and managing a third-party platform vendor, overseeing design approvals (both in-house and outsourced), developing interactive content like quizzes and games, and coordinating production logistics. I ensured the final experience aligned with brand expectations and delivered an engaging, easy-to-access event.

Campaign Results & Brand Impact:

  • Over 700+ registrations within a short promotional period
  • More than 400 attendees joined the live virtual event
  • High engagement maintained throughout the event duration
  • Virtual lobby and auditorium received the highest traffic and interaction
  • Participants actively explored the product booth, selfie booth, and gaming zone
  • Branded hashtag use and in-game rewards sparked active participation
  • Successfully delivered a tech-driven, interactive launch experience despite COVID-19 constraints
  • Met client expectations and achieved overall satisfaction with execution and turnout
